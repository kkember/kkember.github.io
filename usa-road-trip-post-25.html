USA Road Trip - Post #25

Ken Kember

A chilly 5 degreees this morning!  We had a leisurely morning as we only had about 4.5 hours to drive today.  We got away around 9:30 heading east again the I-80.  We stopped for lunch at a rest area - these are so handy for washroom breaks, changing drivers, and having lunch.  They seem to be spaced every 40 miles or so along some interstate highways.  They aren't usually very busy, unlike EnRoutes along the 401.  They have nice spots to have lunch, but it was really windy so we ate in the van.

We arrived at our hotel in Des Moines Iowa around 2pm and were able to check in early.  We drove to a nearby Cariboo Coffee for our afternoon coffee and then headed to "Fabrics and Fun" (more fun for Janet than me :)).  

Driving back we stopped at a christian bookstore - a more normal one this time - no wigs!  We had supper at a Cracker Barrel - first time on this trip.  This brought back memories of our trip to Florida with Mitch and Amy many years ago where we ate at Cracker Barrel many times!

The Cracker Barrel store is in full christmas mode...

Tomorrow we have another fairly short drive to Washington Illinois, just outside Chicago.  The odometer shows that we have now crossed the 10,000 km mark - we are at 10,464 km as of today.
