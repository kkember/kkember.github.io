USA Road Trip - Post #12

Ken Kember

Today was lovely quiet day at Mitch's place with no driving!  We did all our laundry in the morning, and in the afternoon we went for a walk on the trails near his place.  

We followed the trail to a nearby park where there were lots of families and kids playing.

There was a little lending library on the way...

There were some beautiful roses along the way...

Mitch made scones this afternoon and we had them with tea.  For supper he made pasta with marinara sauce, and crepes for dessert - we are eating well!  Tomorrow we will be up bright and early at 6:30 to join the zoom meeting in Ottawa at 9:30 EST.
