Battle Island 2022 - Day 4

Ken Kember

A beautiful day with the sun burning the fog off the water.  Lucy and I drove to Schrieber to pick up some milk for the camp and got coffee and brought it back to the Willows.  We headed for the island around 10 am and checked in with Mitch and Amy at their tent.  They had a good night although a little chilly.  Mitch, along with Caitlin and Mr. Stieh made pancakes for everyone.

Lots of activity today with various work projects underway - painting the workshop and also painting the lighthouse!

Mitch, Amy and I took the canoe out for a spin around part of the island.

For supper we had potatoes baked over the fire and chicken on the barbeque - it was delicious!  

We had a campfire to keep warm while waiting for the fireworks as it drops cooler at night.  We sang a few hymns with Chris on the guitar.

Finally around 11 it was time for fireworks!

Then we headed out in the boat with Eric back to the Willows Inn - arriving around midnight!  Glad to see our beds!
