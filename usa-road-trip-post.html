USA Road Trip - Post #28

Ken Kember

Well, we are within striking distance of home now!  We had a scenic 20 minute drive to the meeting in Joliet Illinois.

The assembly there is now called Spring Creek Gospel Hall.  They have a beautiful building completed in 2016.

The auditorium is really nicely done, with high ceilings and big windows on each side.

Several of the believers there remembered Alan and Rhonda (in particular Brent and Kerwin).  It's funny, I remember there being a lot of younger people, but somehow they have all gotten older over the last 27 years!  We met Matt Slabaugh, who is a grandson of John Slabaugh, the preacher.  I remember going to Akron conference with dad and staying at the Slabaugh's place.  It was really nice to be with them for the morning.  After the breaking of bread they have a break, then a gospel meeting.  Then the kids go to sunday school and there is 30 minutes of ministry.  A lot of the christians live a long distance away so having everything Sunday morning works well.

John and Esther Kregel were visiting from Garnavillo Iowa - I think one of their kids lives in Chicago.  We had a nice visit with them - they were wondering how mom was doing and have happy memories of "Uncle Tim".  They remember mom and dad and Uncle Shad and Aunt Anne staying with them on one of their US trips out to the west coast.

We left around 12:30 and headed east again for Port Huron.  We arrived around 8pm local time - losing another hour so now we are back on Ottawa time, and in sight of the border!
