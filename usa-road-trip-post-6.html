USA Road Trip - Post #6

Ken Kember

We were up early this morning - with the time change we are waking up about 5am.  We had breakfast in the hotel and then headed for the meeting in Sun River, a small town about 20 minutes from Great Falls.  The meeting is comprised of a number of people who have left Hutterite colonies in the area along with some others, and lots of kids!  Joe and Penny Clark kindly invited us to their place for lunch.  They live on the top of a hill above the town, with fields and blue sky all around.

We left their house about 2:30 to head back to Great Falls.  A beautiful drive - horses, cattle, some sheep...

The scenery here is spectacular everywhere...

Back at our hotel, I had a nap, and then we walked over to the grocery store next door to get some supplies for tomorrow, as well as something for supper tonight, although we don't need much after a big lunch.  We hope to make an early start tomorrow for the drive through Glacier National Park.
