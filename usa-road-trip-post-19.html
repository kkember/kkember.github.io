USA Road Trip - Post #19

Ken Kember

Here is what our cabin looked like for last night at Bright Angel Lodge...it was small but very comfortable, and less than 100 yards from the canyon rim.

We got back to the lodge and decided to take the Bright Angel Trail down into the Canyon.  We thought about trying to get to the canyon floor, but changed our minds when we realized that was a ten mile hike - that was just going down.  Lots of signs warned that coming back up takes twice as long - so we decided to just head down for 20-30 minutes and then head back up.  It is a series of switchbacks with steep drop offs, and we were glad we didn't go much further....

You can see the Colorado River in this photo...

We left the park around 12:30 and headed for Kayenta Arizona.  Smooth driving until we hit a town called Tuba City - a major traffic jam where we were stuck for two hours.  It seemed like some kind of Navajo event was going on with very little traffic control or organization so we inched along and finally got clear of it and arrived in Kayenta around 8pm local time (an hour later than California, two hours earlier than Ottawa).  Tomorrow we have a lengthy drive to Denver so hope to make an early start!
