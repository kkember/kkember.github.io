USA Road Trip - Post #26

Ken Kember

A chilly 2 degrees out this morning as we left Des Moines and headed east towards Washington Illinois.  We decided to take secondary roads rather than the interstate since we didn't have too far to go.  We are starting to see some fall colours along the way. 

We crossed over the Mississippi River into Illinois.  Lots of farmland...

We got to the small town of Washington around 2pm.  Janet wanted to visit the Peddler's Way Quilt shop there.

Afterwards we walked around the town square - a really pretty place..

We had coffee at a cafe that used to be a bank - you can see the safe door in the background.

We drove to our hotel in nearby Peoria and checked in.  For supper we ate at a nearby Chick-Fil-A...

Tomorrow we have a short drive to Joliet, Illinois - we plan to go to the breaking of bread at Spring Creek Gospel Hall located there on Sunday.
