The Road Ahead
July 4, 2022

Battle Island 2022 Day 1

Ken Kember

We packed up on Saturday and headed out Sunday morning from Ottawa - a beautiful morning.  We listened to the Breaking of Bread from Ottawa enroute.  Amy drove a good part of the way.

We stopped in Mattawa for lunch at a pretty little rest area - the same place that Amy and I stopped last year.  We had brought stuff for lunch, so had croissants with ham and tomates and macaroni salad, with tea.  It was such a clear blue sky, just a few clouds.

We headed on, stopping at Tim's in Sudbury, and then for gas in Blind River.  I drove from Sudbury onwards, and we arrived at the Sault around 7pm.  We checked into our hotel and then headed for Giovanni's for supper based on Bruce's recommendation - it was very good!

Back to the hotel, then Janet and I headed out to walk down to where her grandmother lived on Woodward Avenue.  Janet spent her summers during high school here helping her grandma run the bookstore - Scripture Gift and Book.  We saw the old hospital where Janet was born, seeminly abandoned now, a sad sight.

We walked on to grandma's house at 126 Woodward - it hasn't changed too much.

Then we walked to Scripture Gift and Book Shop, a walk Janet and her grandma made every day during those summers.  The book shop is still going after all these years!

Still light out even at 10 pm!  Back to our hotel and to bed after a very full day.
