USA Road Trip - Post #29

Ken Kember

Very thankful to be home again after 28 days and 12,537 km of travel!  God is good.  The Odessey held up well, and we were blessed with good weather and safe travelling.

We left our hotel in Port Huron at 7am this morning while it was still dark.  Happy to see no lineup at customs.

A friendly customs officer asked us how the trip went, and then waved us on through.  On to the 402 through to the 401 and to Ottawa, with just a couple of quick stops.  It looks like the fall colors are at their peak here.

We arrived home around 3pm - so nice to see familiar territory.  Everything is in good shape at 874 Clearcrest -  Amy took good care of the house while we were gone, even raking the leaves!  

She had us over for a delicious supper - really nice way to ease back into home life again!

I'm planning one more post tomorrow to wrap up this series - a little bit about our daily routine on the trip, and some overall reflections.
