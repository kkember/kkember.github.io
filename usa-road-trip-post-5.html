USA Road Trip - Post #5

Ken Kember

No shopping today - we were on the road by 9 am.  We headed out on the I-94 West and took the exit for 200S North towards Great Falls.  This is a two-lane road with the occasional passing lane.  Here the speed limit was only 70 mph!  Quite varied landscape ranging from rolling hills, to rocky desert-like gullies to flat plains with hills off on the horizon.

They have several rest areas along the way with a place to pull off, a picnic table or two, and restrooms.

We saw lots of cattle on the hills, several ranches, and the occasional cowboy on horseback!

We arrived in Great Falls around 3:30pm.  It was nice to get into our hotel before supper and be able to relax a little.  Driving 5-6 hours a day doesn't sound like much but when you do it day after day it becomes tiring!  We plan to stay here in Great Falls tonight (Saturday night) as well as Sunday night.  Tomorrow we plan to go the breaking of bread in Sun River, about 20 minutes away.  Joe and Penny Clark have kindly invited us for lunch after the meeting there.  Monday we plan to drive through Glacier National Park on the "Going to the Sun" road which winds through the park.
