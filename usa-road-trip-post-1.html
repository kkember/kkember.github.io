The Road Ahead
September 20, 2022

USA Road Trip  - Post #1

Ken Kember

We finished packing this morning and left 874 Clearcrest just before 9am.  We thought rush hour would be over in Ottawa but not so - took a while to make it through the city.  Light misty rain falling as we left the city.

The skies cleared as we headed northwest along highway 17 towards Renfrew.  We had a brief stop for gas in Cobden and then carried on to a roadside stop near Mattawa - we've stopped here several times when heading to Thunder Bay.  It is a nice quiet spot to have lunch.

After lunch Janet drove and I had a nap.  North Bay, Sturgeon Falls, Sudbury... we stopped in Blind River for an ice cap at Tim Hortons... and then on to the Soo.  We got to our hotel on the Great Northern Road around 7pm - checked in and noticed that Giovanni's almost across the street, so walked over there for supper.

  Giovanni's was right full - a busy place on a Tuesday night!  We walked back to the hotel very full.  Tomorrow we hope to visit the christian book shop where Janet worked with her grandmother back in high school, and then cross over into the US and head for Duluth!
