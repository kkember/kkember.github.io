England 2023 - Day 2

Ken Kember

A quiet day today catching up with the time change.  It's really warm here again today - heading up to 32.  The heat wave is supposed to end by Monday.  We didn't do much this am, had an early lunch and then took the subway over to Marks & Spencer on Kensington High Street.  We picked up supper from the M&S food hall, and I walked back to our airbnb through Holland Park - here are a few photos from along the way...

After getting home I had my second nap of the day :).  Janet stayed longer at M&S and took the subway back.  Supper here, then coffee on the balcony watching the sun go down.  

Tomorrow we plan to go to Bermondsey Gospel Hall for the breaking of bread - it is about a 30-40 minute subway ride.  With an 11 am start time for the meeting, we won't need to be in a hurry tomorrow morning!
