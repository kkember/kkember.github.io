USA Road Trip - Post #21

Ken Kember

We had an enjoyable quiet day today after all the driving yesterday.  A leisurely morning, cleaned out the van, lunch, a nap.  Then I drove Janet to Fancy Tiger Crafts Co-op, about 15 minutes away.  While she was there I checked out Rockler Woodworking which was nearby - lots of neat stuff, sort of like Lee Valley but more exclusively focused on woodworking.

Back at the condo had coffee on the balcony - it's a great place to sit and read.

Carolyn and Steve had invited us over for supper - their house is about 10 minutes from our condo.

We had a really nice supper with them - it's been a long time since we've seen Carolyn and we hadn't met Steve or Natalie and Vanessa, so it was so nice to spend time with them this evening!
