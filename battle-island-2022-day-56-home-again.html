Battle Island 2022 - Day 5/6 - Home again!

Ken Kember

We said goodbye to Bruce and Eric on Thursday morning and were on the road around 9:15 headed east.  We stopped in Schreiber for coffee at a Robin's Donut shop there (not much else!) and drove on towards the Soo.  We had lunch at Subway in Wawa, and were in the Soo by 3:30 and stopped for gas there.

I'm always surprised at how little civilization there seems to be on the north shore of Lake Superior - small towns here and there, but mostly miles of trees and rocks, with the occasional abandoned motel or service centre.  Mitch drove from the Soo to Blind River and then I drove to Sudbury.  We arrived there around 7:30, got some supper and stopped at Dairy Queen for ice cream.  A very nice hotel there - we were in bed by 10pm.  

Up at 7 the next morning, breakfast in our hotel and on the road by 8am.  Amy drove to North Bay, and then I drove from there.  We pulled into Amy's place around 2pm and dropped them off and then home to 874 Clearcrest.  Nice to travel but nice to be home again!  And thankful for many miles safely travelled.

The crew at Battle Island will be packing up today - looks like nice weather for it.  They'll be a tired bunch tonight for sure!  It looks like they did get the lighthouse painted and it looks great!  Below are some pictures Nick and Eric took of the lighthouse and group.
