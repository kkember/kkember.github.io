Battle Island 2022 - Day 3

Ken Kember

It was lightly raining this am and very foggy.  We checked out of our cabin and met up with Eric at the dock at 11 am.  Soon we were on our way to the island amidst the fog.

It was still misty and raining when we landed and headed up to the house.  It was nice to see Christopher and meet Allison and Jack and Calvin for the first time!  I think they had a bit of a wet night with the rain and a leaky tent!

Mitch and Amy set up their tent on the beach - should be a nice view.

Lots of kids running around enjoying the freedom of going whereever they want.  We were hoping the rain would stop and the skies would clear.

The day gradually cleared and by afternoon the sun was shining - nice for everyone to get their tents dried out.  We climbed up into the lighthouse and enjoyed the panoramic view of the island.

As always the kids enjoy checking out all the rocks and the shoreline.

The kids have fun playing together and grabbing snacks from the house.

Mitch and Janet spent some time with little Madeline!  She is very happy and content!

The EDC board gave me a cowboy hat when I was in Calgary - it looks better on Janet than me and keeps the sun off!

You can see Victoria and the two Nicks in this picture, along with Chris on the right.  The woman standing is Gabriel Ranta - friends of Lucy and Bruce who came along with their four kids. 

Mitch and I climbed to a rocky outcrop that overlooks the house and the lighthouse.

We headed back with Eric and Lucy around 9pm to the Willows bed and breakfast inn for the night - enjoyed the day but quite happy to not be tenting!
