July 5, 2022

Battle Island 2022 - Day 2

Ken Kember

We had breakfast in the hotel and then headed out, with stops at the grocery store to get some things for lunch and supper tonight, and at Mark's WorkWearhouse to get a fleece for Janet and hats for Amy and I.  Beautiful drive north of Sault Ste Marie, overcast but no rain yet.  We stopped at Old Woman Bay for lunch on the beach.

Then we drove on, with a quick stop in Wawa at Tims.  As we got closer to Rossport, light rain began to fall.  We pulled in to the Rossport Inn and Cabins, and settled in to our cabin for the night.  We had the largest cabin which has a cute kitchen with everything needed for meals.  It is rustic, but everything is clean and functional!  The train tracks are about 30 feet from our window so we'll hear that later tonight for sure!

After supper, Mitch and I walked down to the dock and met up with Bruce there who had just come back from the island for the night with Eric.  Eric was doing some emails at the Willows Inn and then heading back out before it got too dark.  We arranged to meet at the dock mid-morning tomorrow.
