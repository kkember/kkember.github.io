USA Road Trip - Post #13

Ken Kember

Another quiet day with no driving!  Janet and I were up at 6 to join the zoom meeting in Ottawa at 6:30 our time (9:30 Ottawa time).  We also enjoyed Aaron's ministry session at 8:45/11:45 on Mitch's big screen.

We walked about 15 minutes to a spot called Heavenly Bistro for brunch.  A busy place and we had to wait a while to get seated, but it was worth it!  French toast, eggs, bacon and sausage - well fueled for the day!

Another beautiful day here - the weather seems pretty reliable at 20-25 degrees and sunny.  We took this bridge over one of the busy streets - it connects up with the BART - Bay Area Rapid Transit system.  Mitch uses this to get into the office in San Francisco, about 40 minutes away.

Lots of greenery and a pleasant place to walk around.  

We swam in the condo pool outside Mitch's patio and sat reading and enjoying the sun.  The palm trees seem impossibly tall - not sure how they stay up there!

Tomorrow afternoon we plan to head to Yosemite National Park with Mitch, about 3.5 hours away.  He has been there a couple of times before so can show us around.  We plan to stay overnight near the park and head back to Walnut Creek on Tuesday.
